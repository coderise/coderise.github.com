About

Mission

Our mission is to empower high-school students with demonstrated potential by giving them the tools and inspiration to dive into tech innovation and tap into their entrepreneurial spirit.

Objectives

Graduate 15 students from the Coderise pilot program

Launch the first Coderise Hackathon at the end of the program

Achieve a basic level of Javascript for 70 percent of students

Impact

Coderise provides foundations for the students to participate in:

  • Local accelerators

  • Local technology firms

  • International tech companies

  • Building their own products and bringing their ideas to life


As a result, students will be able to:

  • Stay in school

  • Contribute to their families

  • Land scholarships and create income to pay for school


Coderise is a program free of cost for the students. Weekly transportation and meals will be covered.
